Performance Max

Performance Max - no active campaigns

1 Performance Max campaign exists in the account ("Performance Max Toothpaste") but it's been paused since before the 90-day audit window with zero recent spend.

Performance Max at current spend level
At $13-15K/month in total spend, the account sits in the range where a well-structured Performance Max campaign could supplement Shopping coverage - particularly for display, YouTube and Gmail placements that Shopping doesn't reach. However, adding Performance Max before fixing the tROAS Shopping efficiency issues (HERBAL drag, missing negatives, budget constraint) would spread optimisation signals thin. The recommended sequence: fix Shopping first, then test Performance Max in month 2 or 3 if the Shopping ROAS holds above 3.5x after the efficiency fixes.
If you do activate Performance Max: separate it from Shopping products
Running Performance Max with "all products" while also running a Shopping campaign creates competition between your own campaigns for the same searches. Use a listing group filter in Performance Max to target only products not already in Shopping, or run Performance Max for brand awareness (video/display) with a separate asset group that doesn't overlap with Shopping's product feed.
