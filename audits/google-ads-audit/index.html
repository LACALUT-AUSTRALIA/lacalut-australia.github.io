Performance Max

Performance Max breakdown

All Performance Max campaigns were paused during the audit window. No active Performance Max spend to analyse.

Performance Max Toothpaste is paused - $0 spend in 90 days
"Performance Max Toothpaste" exists in the account structure but generated 0 spend, 0 impressions, and 0 conversions in both the 30-day and 90-day windows. It was likely paused when the Shopping tROAS campaign was activated. This is the right call - running Performance Max and a Shopping campaign on the same products causes internal competition for the same searches. The Shopping tROAS campaign should be allowed to stabilise above the 3.2x target before a Performance Max trial is considered.
When to consider activating Performance Max
Performance Max makes sense at this account once three conditions are met: (1) Shopping tROAS campaign is consistently delivering above 3.5x ROAS over a 30-day period, (2) monthly spend exceeds $20,000/month with data to inform smart bidding, and (3) you have sufficient creative assets - at minimum 3 images, 1 video, 5 headlines, and 5 descriptions per asset group. At current scale (~$10,000 - $15,000/month), the Shopping campaign provides more control and is the right vehicle.
