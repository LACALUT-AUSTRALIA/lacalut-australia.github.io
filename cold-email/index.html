Phase 2 — Warmup (4 Weeks Minimum — Do Not Skip)

Warmup runs automatically in the background. Start: 5–15 emails/day. After 4 weeks you can begin cold sends at 20/day per mailbox.

Phase 3 — Pre-Launch
Clean all lists through NeverBounce or ZeroBounce
Raw lists of 10k likely have 10–15% bad addresses. Bounce rate above 3% damages sender reputation. Cost: ~$40 USD for 10k.

NuReply

One campaign per audience. Use sequences from the Email Sequences tab. Start at 20 emails/day per mailbox — ramp by 10/day each week up to 60–80/day max.
